About STEMSeeds

Our Mission

At STEMSeeds, our mission is to spark curiosity in young learners by making STEM education engaging, approachable, and youth-oriented. We strive to make it accessible for all by offering learning opportunities. We aim to empower the next generation not only to learn STEM—but to envision their future in it. Through student-led teaching and community involvement, we hope to plant a more inclusive future in science, technology, engineering, and math.

About Us

STEMSeeds is a student-led nonprofit founded by three high school students who are passionate about spreading access to STEM education. We work together to design and lead fun hands-on activities and programs to introduce youth to STEM.

As co-founders, we work towards bringing a mix of strengths and perspectives that allow us to create unique experiences that reflect the creativity of STEMSeeds to plant seeds for the future.

Meet The Founders

Nandish Panchal
Chief Technology Officer
As the founder and Chief Technology Officer of STEMSeeds, Nandish combines a lifelong love for technology with a drive to make STEM exciting and approachable for everyone. He first started coding at eight years old, and that early spark has grown into a passion for using tech to bring ideas to life. At STEMSeeds, Nandish leads everything from building the website to creating systems that keep the organization running smoothly. He believes technology is more than just tools and code—it’s a bridge that can connect people to opportunities and ideas they never imagined. Through his work, he hopes to inspire the next generation of creators, problem-solvers, and innovators.
Mahi Patel
Chief Marketing Officer
As our Chief Marketing Officer, Mahi oversees all of the company's marketing efforts, including outreach planning, social media management, and partnership building. She is an accomplished storyteller and communicator who is enthusiastic about advancing STEM education and building deep community ties. Mahi's strong commitment to service is demonstrated by the more than 10 hours she spends each week volunteering with the elderly in addition to her professional role. Through her support of STEM, she is equally committed to helping younger generations, bridging the age divide with empathy and purpose. At the core of her work is her commitment to community involvement and effective communication.
Dviti Soni
Chief Financial Officer
Bringing a thoughtful and mission-driven approach to her role as Chief Financial Officer. With a strong attention to detail and a passion for making education accessible, she ensures that financial resources are used efficiently to support and expand the organization’s impact. Dviti oversees budgeting, funding strategy, and operational planning, always keeping long-term sustainability in focus. Her work plays a vital role in bringing the nonprofit’s vision for free STEM education to life, one smart decision at a time.
