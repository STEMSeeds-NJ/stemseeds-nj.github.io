About STEMSeeds

Our Mission

At STEMSeeds, our mission is to spark curiosity in young learners by making STEM education engaging, approachable, and youth-oriented. We strive to make it accessible for all by offering free learning opportunities. We aim to empower the next generation not only to learn STEM—but to envision their future in it. Through student-led teaching and community involvement, we hope to plant a more inclusive future in science, technology, engineering, and math.

About Us

STEMSeeds is a student-led nonprofit founded by three high school students who are passionate about spreading access to STEM education. We work together to design and lead fun hands-on activities and programs to introduce youth to STEM.

As co-founders, we work towards bringing a mix of strengths and perspectives that allow us to create unique experiences that reflect the creativity of STEMSeeds to plant seeds for the future.
